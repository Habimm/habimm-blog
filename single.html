November 1, 2015 Jane Doe

I don't know what to do with myself. In which cushion to stomp myself in. How to separate the two souls within myself. And how to take off ALL of my clothes.

Truly: This is my only question: How can I strip myself naked? Naked of beliefs? Naked of "common sense"? Naked of EVERYTHING that plagues my heart every single day.

I just want to let go, but can't... I just want to fly, but can't... I just want to be free, but can't quite shake the feeling that this freedom... it will kill me.

Okay, you're sick of this, too, aren't you? Listening to me talk about these "luxurious problems". And I would shut up, if only I was happy...

It's the clouds that plague me every day. It's the monsters that keep me awake each night. It's the UNKNOWN that draws me.

I can't sleep without imagining myself on this "other" side: Where am I? Who am I? Please, rip my heart out! So, I can go and replace it with a robotic one. PLEASE!!?!

I'm crying, but NOBODY listens. Am I alone? Most certainly, I am not. But I can't see the others, only sense them. I know, my friends are out there. Waiting for me to save them. But I have no wings.
